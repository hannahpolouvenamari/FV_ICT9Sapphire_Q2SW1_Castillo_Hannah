Favorite Dog Breeds
Pembroke Welsh Corgi The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a small, powerful, athletic, and active little herder who is friendly and companionable but not needy. They are one of the most popular herding breeds in the world.
Samoyed Samoyeds are large yet elegant smiling dogs that range in size from 19 to slightly more over 23 inches at the shoulder. Sammies are stunningly beautiful but incredibly functional. They are powerful, relentless, and have a thick all-white coat that is impenetrable to cold.
Pekingese The Pekingese, a tiny toy companion with regal posture and a characteristic rolling stride, is one of several breeds developed for ancient China's ruling elite. These are smart canines with unwavering loyalty and a variety of subtle joys. Pekingese are small, stocky toy dogs that can weigh up to 14 pounds.
Beagle In addition to Beagle being a wonderful hunting dog and devoted friend, but it is also cheerful, amusing, and adorable due to its imploring look. Because they were trained to hunt in packs, they like company and are typically laid-back.
Bichon Frise The little but robust and persistent Bichon Frise is one of the world's great 'personality dogs,' relying on charm, beauty, and intellect to weather history's ups and downs since antiquity. A healthy Bichon will stand just under a foot tall at the shoulder.
